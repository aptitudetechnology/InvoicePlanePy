Welcome to InvoicePlane Python

This is the Python/FastAPI version of InvoicePlane, a modern invoicing application.

Getting Started
  • Use the Dashboard to get an overview of your business
  • Manage your Clients to keep track of customer information
  • Create Quotes to send estimates to potential customers
  • Generate Invoices for completed work
  • Record Payments to track what has been paid
  • Manage your Products catalog for easy invoicing
Quick Actions

Use the Quick Actions bar below the navigation to quickly:

  • Add Client - Register a new customer
  • Create Quote - Send an estimate
  • Create Invoice - Bill for completed work
  • Enter Payment - Record received payments
